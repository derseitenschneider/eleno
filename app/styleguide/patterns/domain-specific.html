Usage Guidelines

Note Colors

Use consistent color meanings: Green for success/positive, Yellow for reminders, Blue for information, Red for important/urgent items.

Student Privacy

Use initials for avatar placeholders. Don't display sensitive information in public views. Homework links should be temporary and expire.

Progress Indicators

Always show percentage alongside progress bars. Use consistent colors (primary for progress, background200 for remaining).

Mobile Adaptations

Cards stack vertically on mobile. Use drawer patterns instead of modals. Ensure touch targets are at least 44x44px.
